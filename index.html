Great British Insulation Scheme

GBIS Criteria

The GBIS scheme targets low-income households, with a focus on those receiving means-tested benefits or residing in inefficient social housing.

Eligibility is determined by factors such as:

  • An Energy Performance Certificate (EPC) rating of D or lower for owner-occupiers
  • An EPC rating of E or lower for social housing
  • An EPC rating of D or E for privately rented properties

For the General Group comprising 80% of households, eligibility hinges on:

  • Council Tax bands
  • Standard Assessment Procedure (SAP) ratings

Benefits of the scheme include:

  • Insulation for owner-occupiers
  • Specialized upgrades for social housing with an EPC rating of D
  • Insulation services (excluding cavity wall and loft) for privately rented properties with an EPC rating of D or E.
